Follow the rhythm

Outside of design, music is one of my biggest passions. This project consisted of creating a digital design for the lyrics of one of my favourite songs. The song I chose was "Youth" by Shawn Mendes. This song as an amazing message behind it and I wanted to do it justice through my design.

“When we talk about the word youth, we’re not describing age, but the feeling of happiness and freedom,”
- Shawn Mendes
